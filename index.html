METANOIA

Co-Writer & Co-Director.

Metanoia is a graduation film which was co-created by a group of six students at Supinfocom in 2015. I was responsible for Storyboarding, Animatics, Editing, Animation Direction & Technical Direction for the film.

Metanoia takes us on a magical land as Mr. Philip narrates his epic adventures to kids at a hospital. When unusual events start taking place, we discover that the adventures are more than just bedtime stories.

MALEVOLENT

Writer & Director.

Malevolent is my Debut Film. It was born out of a class project at Supinfocom in 2014. The project was a learning process for students to carry out the entrire process of CG film-making individually. I chose this opportunity to voice myself regarding an rising issue.

In 2013, the high increase of rapes in India caught attention nationwide. People revolted against it, especially after the brutal & shocking incident of "Nirbhaya". But what goes unnoticed is that there are a lot of other henious acts that are still practiced at large in the country.
